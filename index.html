Our Story

A Family Recipe Passed Down

Growing up in a close-knit nuclear family, Ammu often heard stories from her grandmother about Vavamma, her great grandmother, and the beautiful food traditions she created in the family. In those days, tea time was always accompanied by freshly prepared snacks from the kitchen. Vavamma prepared everything with great care and lots of love.

As the years passed, Ammu’s grandmother, affectionately known as Pushpa Kunjamma , felt that these flavours were too special to remain only within the family. With courage and determination, she began making and selling their traditional snacks is small packs.

For the next 25 years she slowly built the business with care and dedication. Every batch of Achappam and Kuzhalappam carried a piece of her love and effort. She named the business Ammu’s Food Products after her dear granddaughter,with the hope that the family legacy would continue for generations.

Today, that journey continues with the same pride and gratitude. While much has evolved and the menu has expanded, the core products still follow the old recipes that were passed down in our family.No preservatives, no shortcuts, only time, patience and honest tradition.
